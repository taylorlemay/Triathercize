BIKE >>

Biking is a very popular and effective cardio workout because it allows you to not only have a choice between cycling indoors or outdoors, it also keeps your heart rate elevated while working several different muscles of your body.

Materials:

  • Bicycle
  • Headphones
  • A Good Music Playlist

Steps:

  1. START with a warm up
    • Before cycling, you should take at least five minutes to stretch your muscles out.
    • Biking makes you use a lot of different parts of your body so it's important to stretch prior to working out to prevent pulling any muscles.
  2. BEGIN your workout
    • Start by using your bicycle at a low incline and a low gear.
    • After 5-10 minutes, gradually increase your gears to create more tension.
    • Continue to switch gears during different intervals to prevent muscle memory from occuring, changing them when the incline decreases or increases as well.
  3. END with a cool down
    • Bring your gears back to the lowest level and bike for a few minutes at a 0% incline.
    • This part of your workout is crucial because your muscles need time to cool off and gradually lower your heart rate.

Additional Tips:

  • If you are not stationed at a cycle machine, be sure to find a bike path outside that does not just have one type of incline.
  • If you are using a stationary bike, don't forget to change the incline during your different intervals.
