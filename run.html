RUN >>

Running is one of the most basic and essential cardio workouts that exist. Having the strength to run is very important for your body's overall health.

Materials:

  • Sneakers
  • Headphones
  • A Good Music Playlist

Steps:

  1. START with a warm up
    • Just like cycling, you should take at least five minutes to stretch your muscles out.
    • Running uses numerous different parts of your body so it's important to stretch prior to working out to prevent pulling any muscles.
  2. BEGIN your workout
    • Start with a light jog and gradually increase your incline and pace.
    • To prevent your muscles from getting used to the same pace, plan out different intervals of time that you either run slow or fast. This will keep your heart rate elevated and help you get the most out of your workout.
  3. END with a cool down
    • Take another five minutes at the end of your run to stretch out your muscles again.
    • This part of your workout is crucial because your muscles need time to cool down and stretch to prevent cramping and other health problems.

Additional Tips:

  • No matter how tired you may get, don't forget to breathe. Often times, runners hold their breath for certain periods of time, but to get the most out of your workout, you want to find a breathing pattern that works best for you.
  • To keep yourself motivated, try to play music while working out that has a fast beat and strong rhythm.
